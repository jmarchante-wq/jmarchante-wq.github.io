01 — About

Science as a craft,
data as a language.

I am a drug discovery scientist with deep expertise in in vitro pharmacology, assay development, and translational biology. At iTeos Therapeutics I founded both the Screening & Assay Development team and the bioinformatics function — building capabilities that supported multiple assets from early discovery through to the clinic.

My edge is the full stack: I can design an assay, run a screen, analyse the output computationally, and speak the same language as the clinician reviewing the Phase I data. That span is rare, and it tends to matter most at inflection points — when a programme needs direction, not just execution.

I am based in Brussels and open to senior roles across Europe in oncology, immuno-oncology, or any disease area where rigorous in vitro biology meets ambitious translational thinking.

In Vitro Pharmacology

02

Screening & Hit-Finding

HTS campaign design, cascade architecture, hit confirmation and triage. Experience across small molecules, biologics, and combination approaches in immuno-oncology.

03

Bioinformatics & Data Science

04

Translational Interface

Team & Function Building

CRO Management
